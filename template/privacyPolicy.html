Privacy Policy

Twitch Account Details

Crypto Art Games collects some Twitch profile information as part of the standard single sign on process.

  • Account name, display name and profile image

    By participating in Crypto Art Games you agree to have your account name, display name and profile image displayed on the website as part of one of our games. You also agree to have these details included in any works produced and compiled for sale and distribution by Crypto Art Games (ie. Non Fungible Tokens). NFTs sold on OpenSea may contain Twitch account display names and/or profile images. If you do not agree to this then you should not participate.


  • Email Address

    We retrieve email addresses from Twitch as part of the single sign in process. We do not store email address. We store a peppered hash of the normalized address to prevent users from creating duplicate accounts. 
    
email_hash = base64(sha256(pepper + normalize(email)))
    We will never send you email because we don't store your email address.
    We will never share your email address with third parties because we don't store your email address.

Security

Crypto Art Games is owned and operated by web application security experts. Here are a few of the security measures used to safeguard the application and your data within it:

  • SSH password authentication disabled
  • Restrictive Linux firewall configuration
  • Restrictive Network Configuration
  • Restrictive Content Security Policy
  • Restrictive X-Frame-Options
  • Restrictive Cross Domain Policies
  • OAuth 2.1 Single Sign On
  • Secure HttpOnly Session cookies
  • Mandatory two factor authentication on all privileged accounts

If you think you've found a security problem, please contact us admin@cryptoart.games
